Learning Resources

Most of what I have learned in the data space so far has been through self-learning. And here are some of the valuable resources I have come across throughout my journey.

I hope you find them helpful.

Enjoy and never stop learning!



General Data Analysis Content
Alex The Analyst

In his channel, Alex Freberg shares precious tips that both new and more experienced Data Analysts can benefit from. It includes tutorials, portfolio ideas, course recommendations, and much more.

Maven Analytics

Fantastic Data Analytics content delivered in an organised and clear fashion. They have their own platform, but you can also find them in other platforms such as Udemy, LinkedIn Learning and YouTube.

Microsoft Power BI
Curbal

Ruth Pozuelo is a Microsoft Data Platform MVP. Her YouTube channel "Curbal" has tone of information on Power BI, including her view on new features, tips on DAX, M, and so on.

Guy in a Cube

Similarly to Curbal, this channel is likely to have almost everything you need to know about Power BI.

sqlbi

sqlbi is managed by the Power BI experts Marco Russo and Alberto Ferrari. The content they produce is great, especially when it comes to DAX and data modeling.

Microsoft Technologies in general
Microsoft Learn

I use Microsoft Learn for both preparing for certifications and brushing up on certain technologies.

Although I am not a big fan of Microsoft documentation in general, I have to admit that Microsoft has indeed been doing a great job improving its training material and delivery methods.

Microsoft Learn has helped immensily lately. Some of its learning paths even include labs, which is super helpful.

SQL
khan Academy

There are plenty of high quality courses freely available on this platform and SQL basics is one of them.

Python
Curso em Video

For the Portuguese speakers out there willing to get started with Python, "Curso em Video" might be the way to go. It has one of the best (if not the best) free Python courses I have found so far.

They also have courses on other programming languages and technologies.
