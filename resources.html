Learning Resources

Most of what I have learnt in the data space so far has been through self-study. Therefore, I feel obliged to share some good stuff that have been helping me along the way.

I hope they can be as helpful to you as they have been to me.

Enjoy and never stop learning!

General Data Analysis Content
Alex The Analyst

In his channel, Alex Freberg shares precious tips that both new and more experienced Data Analysts can benefit from. It includes portfolio ideas, SQL tutorials, courses recommendations, and much more.

Maven Analytics

Fantastic Data Analytics content delivered in an organised and clear fashion. They have their own platform, but you can also find them in other channels such as Udemy, LinkedIn Learning and YoutTube.

Microsoft Power BI
Curbal

Ruth Pozuelo is a Microsoft Data Platform MVP. Her YouTube channel "Curbal" has tone of information on Power BI, including her view on new features, tips on DAX, M, and so on.

Guy in a Cube

Similarly to Curbal, this channel is likely to have almost everything you need to know about Power BI.

sqlbi

sqlbi is managed by the Power BI experts Marco Russo and Alberto Ferrari. The content they produce is great, especially when it comes to DAX.

Microsoft Technologies
Microsoft Learn

I have gone through some Microsoft Learning Paths and was surprised to see how they have improved their training and documentation.

For instance, these Learning Paths contained most of the information I needed to prepare for the Microsoft exams I have taken. Some of the paths even have some labs.
